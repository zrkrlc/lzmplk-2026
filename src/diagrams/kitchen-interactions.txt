  ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  1. LOOK                          → kitchenLooked=true, reveals all objects

  3. TAKE BOWL                     → BOWL in inventory
  4.  [go to COUNTER]              → TAKE APRON (apron only takeable there)

  7. USE BOWL                      → mascotTrapped=true  "CLANG!"
  8. USE APRON                     → ★ WIN ★

  - KNIFE, BAG, TREATS are collectable but all red herrings
  - OPEN DRAWER leads only to BAG (red herring); skip if playing optimally
  - Apron is acquired at COUNTER (revealed on first LOOK there), not in KITCHEN
